## object naming

Can't use "." (dots) as separators in the name, switched to underscore instead

### template

* `<PhysicalObjectType>_<ROom>_<designation>`
* `<PhysicalObjectType>_<ROom>[-<specifics>]_<designation>`

## Explanation

* LO = Lounge
* LI = Living Room
